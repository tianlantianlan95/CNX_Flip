
*** Add a card ***
curl -H "Content-Type: application/json" -X POST -d '{"deckid":1,"term":"newTerm", "definition":"new def"}' http://localhost:5000/api/cards

*** Edit a card 
curl -H "Content-Type: application/json" -X PUT -d '{"id":1,"term":"hahaha", "definition":"hahaha"}' http://localhost:5000/api/cards

*** Delete a card ***
curl -H "Content-Type: application/json" -X DELETE -d '{"id": 29}' http://localhost:5000/api/cards

*** Add a deck ***
curl -H "Content-Type: application/json" -X POST -d '{"title":"deck_title", "color":"blue", "card":[]}' http://localhost:5000/api/decks

*** Edit a deck ***
curl -H "Content-Type: application/json" -X PUT -d '{"id":1, "title": "aloha", "color":"white", "card":[]}' http://localhost:5000/api/decks

*** Delete a deck ***
curl -H "Content-Type: application/json" -X DELETE -d '{"id":1}' http://localhost:5000/api/decks
