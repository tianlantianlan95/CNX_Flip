Welcome to OpenStax Flip

What is OpenStax Flip?

OpenStax Flip will provide our textbook users with an app to enhance their mastery of content by practicing retrieval techniques.

What are Problems?

Auxiliary contents are not easily accessible to students.Besides, Students who doesn't join a Tutor class lack practice materials.

What are Solutions?

To develop a web & mobile app to supplement content from textbooks and Tutor, and serve as an interface for students to practice.
